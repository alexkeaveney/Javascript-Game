How to play

  • The cards are dealt out evenly. Remainders are put in the pile.
  • The game starts from the left of the first person in the lobby.
  • The first card is Ace. The player must put down 1 - 4 cards face down claiming them all to be Aces or skip their turn. None of them actually have to be Aces.
  • The other players are then given the chance to call 'cheat' on the player if they don't believe the player has that many Aces.
  • The cards are then flipped around. If the player is caught lying they must take all of the cards from the pile.
  • If the player told the truth the player(s) that called cheat on them must take all of the cards in the pile.
  • The next card will be 2. The player to the left must claim 2's or skip their turn.
  • This goes on 3, 4, 5 .. King and then back to Ace.
  • The object of the game is to get rid of all your cards.
